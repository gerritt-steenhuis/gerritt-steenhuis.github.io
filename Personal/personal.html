About Me

Hi, I'm Gerritt! I am a student at the University of Waterloo in my 3rd year of Mechanical Engineering. I have extensive experience in design (both drafting and web development), and I have a passion for writing the slickest code a Mechanical Engineer can pull off. I love programming in Python, and am proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and React. I have a knack for all things mechanical, and enjoy fixing my car or tinkering with homemade projects. I have a broad skill base with experience ranging from designing playgrounds using AutoCAD 3D, to managing a team implementing a $5.5 million dollar change at Toyota, to creating React Apps that display manufacturing plants' efficiency metrics in realtime. Keep reading to find out more about my projects and experiences, and don't hesitate to contact me.

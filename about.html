A Bit About Who I Am

When I'm not tossing a frisbee, or golfing with friends, you might catch me with a fishing pole in my hand, or up at algonquin park backpacking. I'm a fan of hanging aroung in my hammock, or revamping my homemade survival kits for my next outdoor expedition. I enjoy bouldering, and dream of the chance to climb Squamish or the Yosemite mountains. When in school, I participate on the University of Waterloo Hybrid team, and am always ready to help out a friend when they didn't quite get something. I enjoy taking online courses about subjects that interest me, such as anything programming, or development related.
